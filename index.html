About Us

LG,like any other company, sure has a story behind its current products and endeavors.Though it is having a little trouble competing with companies like Samsung and Apple in the smartphone space,LG is still one of the best-known smartphone manufacturers in the world.According to Statista,LG is the 3rd most popular smartphone maker in the USA with a 9.7% subscriber base(as Febraury,2016).While LG is currently mostly known for its "Life's Good" slogan,that is not the only catchphrase stuck to thier name.They have also used "Future's Technology" from 1997 to 1999,in addition to "Digitally Yours".LG Way aims to achieve LG'S vision of "No.1 LG",by practicing "People-oriented Management",and "Creating values for customers" through "Jeong-Do Management".

Vision

The ultimate goal of LG is to be recognised as the market leader in business perforamce as well as in managemnt practices. To Customers-a trustworthy brand that provides best products and services.To Investors-a secure investment that yields sustained high returns. To Employees-a great workplace that talented individuals want to join.To Competitors-a leader that dominates,but a leader to learn from

Behavioral Mode

Ethical managemnt and code of conduct to enable capacity development and fair competition. Integrity-Work transparently according to principles and standards.Fair Transaction-Provide equal opportunity and fair treatment in every transactional relationship.Fair Competition-Improve capabilities with which one can fairly win the competition

Management Principles

Customer first-Think of customers as the top priority-the starting point pf Management.Make decisions with the emphasis on the end-user.Delivery of substantive value-Be one step ahead in finding latent customer needs.Provide the best products and services that exceed customer expectations.Innovations-driven creation:Come up with differntiated ideas that step"outside the box".Always look for and practice better method and practices.
